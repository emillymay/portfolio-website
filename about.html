About Me

"In the emerging, highly programmed landscape ahead, you will either create the software or you will be the software. It’s really that simple: Program, or be programmed. Choose the former, and you gain access to the control panel of civilization. Choose the latter, and it could be the last real choice you get to make." Douglas Rushkoff – Author, Program or Be Programmed

I call Boulder, Colorado my home yet I enjoy expierencing all sorts of scenery. Wiether by foot, car or plane. My favorite hobby these past few years has been traveling and camping. I have a deep love for nature and our planet. If Im not working on code then you can always find me hiking at some of the most beautiful spots around Boulder, one including the famous Flatirons!
